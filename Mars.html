Mars Stationary Satellite

Plotting Coverage with Leaflet.js



  

    Above should be a map showing the coverage area of a stationary satellite. You can move the satellite left and right to see what all it can cover. Keep in mind what we learned earlier about the stable equilibrium points at -17.92 and 167.83, and the two unstable ones are 254.45 and 75.34 degrees longitude.
